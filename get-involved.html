Get Involved

Evangelism, humanitarian projects, and community development across Cameroon — discover the projects that are transforming lives and how you can be a part of it

Building Project

Across the Eastern region of Cameroon, many communities lack basic infrastructure — no permanent churches, no meeting halls, and no safe gathering spaces. Our Building Project constructs durable structures where communities can worship, hold Bible studies, and come together for fellowship.

These buildings become the heart of each village — a visible sign of hope and a foundation for long-term spiritual growth. Each project is built in partnership with local believers who contribute labor and materials, ensuring ownership and sustainability.

✝️

Evangelize 138 Surrounding Villages

In the jungles of Eastern Cameroon, 138 villages surrounding our base have never heard the Gospel. Many of these communities are only accessible by foot through dense forest trails. Our evangelism teams travel village to village, spending days in each community — sharing the message of Jesus through personal conversations, open-air gatherings, and film screenings.

In January 2023 alone, our school of evangelism in the Eastern region reached seven villages with over 300 souls won for the Lord. Every Saturday, our headquarters church in Yaoundé conducts outreach in the neighborhoods of Mefou-Assi, Etoa, and the Ahala food market, adding new converts weekly.

💧

Dig Drinking Water Wells

In the remote villages of Eastern Cameroon, families walk hours to collect water from rivers and streams — water that often carries disease. Waterborne illness is one of the leading causes of child mortality in these communities. Our well-digging initiative provides clean, safe drinking water directly within village reach.

Each well serves an entire community, dramatically reducing illness and freeing women and children from daily water-fetching journeys. Clean water opens the door for trust, deeper relationships, and the opportunity to share the Gospel with communities that have been overlooked.

🏥

Build a Health Center

The nearest medical facility for many Eastern Cameroon villages is a full day's journey away. Mothers give birth without medical support, children suffer from treatable illnesses, and preventable diseases claim lives that could be saved. Our health center project will bring basic medical care — consultations, maternal health, first aid, and disease prevention — directly to these underserved communities.

Beyond healthcare, the center will serve as a hub for health education, nutrition programs, and community wellness. Meeting physical needs with compassion opens hearts to the message of the Gospel and demonstrates Christ's love in action.

🏫

Build a Primary School

Most children in the remote villages of Eastern Cameroon have no access to formal education. Without a school nearby, they grow up without literacy, without opportunity, and without the tools to break the cycle of poverty. Our primary school project will give these children their first chance at education — a safe place to learn, grow, and dream.

Through our existing Back-to-School Scholarship program, we already sponsor 25 children across nursery, primary, secondary, and university levels. Building a dedicated school will multiply that impact, reaching hundreds of children and training the next generation of community leaders.
