About Us

Our story, our heart, and the people who make this mission possible

How We Began

Lumière Congo grew out of Jubilee Apostolic Harvest Ministries — a calling placed on the heart of Brother Daniel to reach the unreached. What began as a burden for the families living in the jungles of Eastern Cameroon became a movement of evangelism, church planting, and humanitarian service that now spans multiple regions.

From our headquarters church in Yaoundé, we send teams into the most remote areas of Cameroon — villages accessible only by foot through dense forest trails. In these communities, people have lived for generations without hearing the name of Jesus, without clean water, without schools, and without medical care. We go to them.

Our approach has always been simple: go where the need is greatest, love people where they are, and trust God with the results. Through schools of evangelism, weekly outreach in Yaoundé neighborhoods, the Apostolic Bible College, children's music programs, and back-to-school scholarships, we walk alongside the people we serve — meeting both spiritual and physical needs.

Our Values

Love First: We believe that love is the foundation of all effective ministry. Before we share the Gospel, we seek to understand, to listen, and to love.

Relationship Over Programs: We invest in people, not projects. Our goal is to build lasting relationships that continue long after our teams return home.

Local Partnership: We work alongside local leaders, translators, and community members. We don't come as outsiders bringing solutions, but as partners walking together in faith.

Prayer as Foundation: Every decision, every mission, every relationship is covered in prayer. We believe that prayer is not preparation for the work—it is the work.

2023 Annual Highlights

A look back at what God accomplished through our ministry

Missions

Schools & Training

  • The Apostolic Bible College (ABC) covered two-thirds of their second-level program
  • The music school for kids registered 15 new students into the program

Caritas & Education

Our Team

Brother Daniel

Founder & Lead Missionary

Brother Daniel is the founder of Jubilee Apostolic Harvest Ministries. He leads mission trips across Cameroon and internationally, from the AWAKE Middle East conference in Dubai to outreaches deep in the Eastern Cameroon jungles.

Sister Daniel

Co-Missionary & Ministry Partner

Sister Daniel serves alongside her husband in every aspect of the ministry — from traveling on international mission trips to supporting the headquarters church in Yaoundé and caring for the communities they serve.

ABC Director

Apostolic Bible College

Leads the Apostolic Bible College (ABC) training program, equipping students with deep Biblical knowledge and preparing them for ministry. The college has completed two-thirds of its second-level curriculum.

Music School Lead

Children's Music Program

Oversees the music school for children, nurturing young talent and using music as a bridge to share the Gospel. In 2023, 15 new children were registered into the program.

Eastern Region Lead

Branch Coordinator — Eastern Cameroon

Coordinates all ministry activities at the Eastern Cameroon branch — from village evangelism and the "Let There Be Light" project to building construction and community outreach in the surrounding jungle villages.

Education Coordinator

Back-to-School Scholarship Program

Manages the scholarship program that sponsors orphans, displaced children, and students in need — from nursery through university. In 2024, all 12 sponsored students were promoted to their next grade.
