About Us

Our story, our heart, and the people who make this mission possible

How We Began

Lumière Congo was born from a simple, profound calling. In 2017, a small group of believers felt compelled to go where few others were willing to travel—deep into the heart of the Democratic Republic of Congo, to villages that had never heard the name of Jesus.

What started as a single mission trip has grown into a movement. Over the past eight years, we've seen God work in extraordinary ways—transforming not just individual lives, but entire communities. Villages that once lived in spiritual darkness now have thriving churches. Families that had no hope now know the love of Christ.

Our approach has always been simple: go where the need is greatest, love people where they are, and trust God with the results. We don't have elaborate programs or complex strategies. We have the Gospel, willing hearts, and a commitment to walk alongside the people we serve.

Our Values

Love First: We believe that love is the foundation of all effective ministry. Before we share the Gospel, we seek to understand, to listen, and to love.

Relationship Over Programs: We invest in people, not projects. Our goal is to build lasting relationships that continue long after our teams return home.

Local Partnership: We work alongside local leaders, translators, and community members. We don't come as outsiders bringing solutions, but as partners walking together in faith.

Prayer as Foundation: Every decision, every mission, every relationship is covered in prayer. We believe that prayer is not preparation for the work—it is the work.
