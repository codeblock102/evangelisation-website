Your Impact

300+ Souls Won in the Eastern Region

In January 2023, a school of evangelism and discipleship was held in the Eastern region. Seven villages were evangelized, and over 300 people gave their lives to Christ. Baptism of over 100 converts followed at Easter.

12 Students Sponsored — All Promoted

In 2024, your giving sponsored 12 students from nursery through university. Every single one of them passed their exams and was promoted to the next grade — from King Joshua in nursery to Bih and Senior at university level.

"Let There Be Light" — Solar Torchlights

Families in the jungles of Eastern Cameroon received solar torchlights through our outreach program. Each torchlight costs just $30 and brings both physical and spiritual light to homes that previously had none.

Apostolic Bible College Growth

The Apostolic Bible College (ABC) has completed two-thirds of its second-level program, training future pastors and ministry leaders. Additionally, 15 children were enrolled in the music school in 2023.

Weekly Outreach in Yaoundé

Every Saturday, our headquarters church conducts evangelism outreach in the neighborhoods of Mefou-Assi, Etoa, and the Ahala food market. New converts are added weekly, with a discipleship school held every Thursday for follow-up.
