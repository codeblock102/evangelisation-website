Your gift brings light to villages that have never heard the Gospel

Every dollar you give helps us reach remote villages in the Democratic Republic of Congo with the message of hope. Your support funds mission trips, provides Bibles and resources, trains local leaders, and sustains the ongoing work of planting churches in places where the light of the Gospel has never shone.

Your Impact

Village of Mwamba

Thanks to generous donors, we were able to establish a church in Mwamba village. Today, 45 families gather weekly for worship, and three local leaders have been trained to continue the ministry.

Bible Distribution

Your gifts provided 200 Bibles in local languages to villages across the Eastern Province. Many recipients had never owned a Bible before and now read God's Word daily.

Leader Training

Monthly support enabled us to train 12 local leaders in discipleship and church planting. These leaders are now multiplying the work, reaching their own neighboring villages.

Church Planting Success

Your donations helped establish 8 new churches in remote areas. Each church now has trained leadership and is actively reaching out to surrounding communities with the Gospel.

Children's Ministry Growth

Through your support, we've been able to develop children's programs in 15 villages. Over 300 children now regularly attend Bible classes and learn about Jesus in age-appropriate ways.
