Carrying Light to the Heart of the Congo

Join the Mission

"We go not because it is easy, but because the light must reach those who wait in darkness."

Lumière Congo was born from a simple calling: to bring the Gospel to remote villages in the Democratic Republic of Congo where hope is scarce and light is needed most. Our teams journey deep into the heart of Africa, building relationships, sharing truth, and planting seeds of faith that transform entire communities.

We believe that every person deserves to hear the message of hope in their own language and context. Through partnerships with local churches, community leaders, and dedicated volunteers, we've seen villages transformed—not just spiritually, but in their daily lives as well. The light we bring doesn't just illuminate; it transforms, heals, and restores.

What We Do

✝️

Evangelism

We share the Gospel through personal relationships, village gatherings, and one-on-one conversations, meeting people where they are with love and truth.

🤝

Community Building

Beyond words, we build lasting connections—supporting local churches, training leaders, and walking alongside communities in their journey of faith.

🙏

Prayer Support

Every mission is covered in prayer. We believe in the power of intercession and invite you to join us in lifting up these villages before the Lord. Our prayer network spans continents, connecting hearts across oceans to lift up the work God is doing in the Congo.
