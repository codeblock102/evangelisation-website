Our Mission

Bringing the light of the Gospel to remote villages across the Democratic Republic of Congo

✝️

Evangelism

Our primary calling is to share the Good News of Jesus Christ with those who have never heard. We journey to remote villages, often accessible only by foot or canoe, to bring the message of hope and salvation.

We believe that every person deserves to hear the Gospel in their own language and context. Our teams work alongside local translators and community leaders to ensure the message is understood and received with love.

🤝

Community Building

Evangelism is not just about words—it's about building lasting relationships. We invest in communities by training local leaders, supporting new churches, and walking alongside believers as they grow in their faith.

We help establish Bible study groups, provide discipleship materials, and create networks of support so that new believers can continue to grow even after our teams return home.

"Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation."

— Mark 16:15
🙏

Prayer & Support

Every mission is covered in prayer. We believe that prayer is the foundation of all effective ministry. Before, during, and after each trip, we commit to interceding for the villages we visit, the people we meet, and the seeds of faith being planted.

We also provide ongoing support to the communities we serve, maintaining relationships through regular communication, follow-up visits, and continued prayer partnerships.

🌱

Sustainable Impact

Our goal is not just to visit, but to see lasting transformation. We work to ensure that the Gospel takes root in each community, growing into strong, self-sustaining churches that can continue the work long after we're gone.

We train local leaders, provide resources, and create systems of accountability so that the light we bring continues to shine long into the future. Each village we serve becomes a beacon of hope, radiating the love of Christ to neighboring communities.

📖

Bible Translation & Distribution

We believe that everyone should have access to God's Word in their own language. Our teams work with local translators to provide Bibles and study materials in languages spoken throughout the Congo.

Over the past eight years, we've distributed thousands of Bibles, many to people who had never owned one before. These Bibles become treasured possessions, read daily and shared within families and communities.

🏥

Medical & Practical Care

While our primary mission is spiritual, we recognize that meeting physical needs opens doors for the Gospel. We provide basic medical care, clean water initiatives, and support for education where possible.

These practical expressions of love demonstrate that our faith is not just words, but action. They build trust, create relationships, and show the tangible love of Christ to those we serve.
